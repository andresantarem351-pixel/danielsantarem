Daniel Santarém

About

Daniel Santarém is a research collaborator in Exercise and Health at CIDESD’s Heart community. His main lines of research integrate advanced physiological monitoring techniques to assess the acute and chronic response of biomarkers, with a focus on muscle oxygenation assessed by near-infrared spectroscopy (NIRS). In 2024, he participated in the mNIRS 2024: Muscle Oxygen Bridging Science, Technology, and Practice conference, which resulted in the distinction of best poster presentation.

Daniel Santarém has developed scientific articles published in leading international peer-reviewed journals and has received significant international recognition. He presented in the 27th (Seville, Spain) and 28th (Paris, France) editions of the ECSS Congress, and at the 1st edition of the mNIRS 2024: Muscle Oxygen Bridging Science, Technology, and Practice conference (Colchester, United Kingdom), which resulted in the distinction of best poster presentation. In 2025, he participated as a speaker at the 2025 Moxy Summit Webinar, earning positive feedback from experts in NIRS monitoring.
